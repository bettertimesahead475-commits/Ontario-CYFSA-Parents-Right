Your Charter Rights at a Glance

These rights apply to every CAS investigation in Ontario.

Section 7
Life, Liberty & Security
CAS must follow principles of fundamental justice. You cannot be deprived of your child arbitrarily.
Section 8
Search & Seizure
You can refuse entry to your home without a warrant. CAS needs either consent, a warrant, or an emergency.
Section 9
Arbitrary Detention
You cannot be kept at a CAS office against your will or detained without legal authority.
Section 10(b)
Right to Counsel
You have the right to speak with a lawyer before answering CAS questions. Say so immediately.
Section 15
Equality Rights
CAS cannot discriminate based on race, disability, religion, culture, or socioeconomic status.
