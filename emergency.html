üõ°Ô∏è Your Rights at the Moment of Removal
Right to Know Why

CAS must tell you exactly why your child is being removed and under which CYFSA section. Ask: "Which section 74(2) subsection applies?"

Right to Know Where

CAS must tell you where your child is being placed immediately. Demand the address and contact information of the foster home or placement.

Right to a Lawyer

You have the right to speak with a lawyer before any interviews. Say: "I want to speak with a lawyer. I am calling Legal Aid now: 1-800-668-8258"

Right to a Hearing

Under CYFSA s.84(5), CAS must bring your child before the court within 5 days. You have the right to be present and represented.

Right to Access

Unless the court orders otherwise, you generally have the right to see and speak with your child. Ask CAS immediately about an access schedule.

Right to Kinship

Ask CAS to consider placing your child with a family member (grandparent, aunt/uncle, family friend) instead of a stranger foster home.
